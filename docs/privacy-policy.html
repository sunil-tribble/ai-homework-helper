At AI Homework Helper, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Device ID: A unique identifier for your device used for rate limiting and managing premium status
  • Homework Problems: The questions and images you submit for solving
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about app usage to improve our service
Important: We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details unless you explicitly provide them when contacting support.

How We Use Information

  • To provide step-by-step homework solutions
  • To enforce daily limits for free users (5 problems per day)
  • To manage premium subscriptions and features
  • To improve our AI algorithms and user experience
  • To detect and prevent abuse of our service

Data Storage and Security

Your homework history is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not store your homework problems on our servers after processing is complete.

All data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols to ensure your information remains secure.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: To process homework problems and generate solutions. Their privacy policy applies to data processing.
  • RevenueCat: To manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. No personal data is shared.
  • Apple Analytics: To understand app usage patterns (if you have opted in via iOS Settings).

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can view your homework history within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics in iOS Settings
  • Subscriptions: Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings

Data Retention

We retain processed homework data only for the duration needed to provide the solution. Historical data on your device is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States where our servers and third-party services are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
